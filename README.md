# S68_Capstone_InterestInn

**Vision:**
InterestInn aims to be the ultimate digital space where people explore, learn, and grow their passions through interactive experiences and community engagement. From creative arts like origami and crochet to active pursuits like cycling and hiking — InterestInn connects hobbyists through personalized journeys and collaborative challenges. We don’t just help people learn hobbies — we help them live their passions.
